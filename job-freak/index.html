About Job Freak

Job Freak is a site where you can see lists of companies currently hiring, salary offered, job description and other details.

Guide

This is a guide on how to use this site.

  • Go to Search Page
  • Search for a company
  • Click on the company you want to see
  • You will be redirected to the company's page
  • You can see the company's name, salary offered, job description and other details
  • Click on "Apply Now" to apply for the job
